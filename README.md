Buy Me A Coffee Website-

Welcome to the Buy Me a Coffee website! This platform allows users to support creators by making small donations, typically in the form of buying them a coffee. Whether you're a developer, artist, or content creator, this website is a fun and easy way to receive support from your fans.

Features-

Simple and Clean UI: A user-friendly interface to easily interact with the website.
Donation via PayPal: Support your favorite creators with a simple "Buy Me A Coffee" donation button that links to PayPal.
Responsive Design: Accessible from any device, whether it's a phone, tablet, or desktop.
Customizable: You can personalize your profile page to suit your branding.
Technologies Used
HTML: For the structure of the website.
CSS: For styling and making the website visually appealing.
JavaScript: For interactive elements and dynamic content.
PayPal API: For handling donations and payment processing.

How It Works
Create a Profile: Customize your profile with your name, image, and a short description.
Add PayPal: Set up a PayPal donation button by linking it to your PayPal account.
Share Your Link: Share your personalized "Buy Me a Coffee" link with your supporters so they can donate.
Track Donations: The website shows the number of coffees purchased and the total amount donated.
